## FAQ

<FaqItem question="English or Korean available?">
  International/private ERs often have English. **Translation app + insurer hotline** helps. Try **0800-024-111 foreigner hotline**.
</FaqItem>

<FaqItem question="Direct insurance billing?">
  **Partner hospitals** may bill insurers directly. Otherwise **pay first, claim later** — keep all originals.
</FaqItem>

<FaqItem question="Emergency number?">
  **119** (ambulance/ER). Call immediately for life-threatening symptoms — bring passport and insurance.
</FaqItem>

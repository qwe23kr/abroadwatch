## FAQ

<FaqItem question="Can I fly home the same day I lose my passport in Taipei?">
  **Almost never.** Emergency passports take **1–2 business days**; **Korean Mission in Taipei** intake is **weekdays 09:00–16:00** only. **Reschedule your flight +1–2 days** first.
</FaqItem>

<FaqItem question="How long does an emergency passport take?">
  Usually **1–2 business days** after intake. Always call **+886-2-2758-8299** to confirm current processing time.
</FaqItem>

<FaqItem question="What if I don't have a passport photo?">
  Photo shops near mission — confirm 2-inch (3.5×4.5 cm) spec
</FaqItem>

<FaqItem question="Can I get help on weekends?">
  **district police (110)**: 24/7 for reports. **Korean Mission in Taipei** passport intake: **weekdays 09:00–16:00** — closed weekends/holidays. Weekend loss: police report → reschedule flight → **call mission next business day**.
</FaqItem>

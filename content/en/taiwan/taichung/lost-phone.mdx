## FAQ

<FaqItem question="Can I block the SIM without the phone?">
  **Yes.** Call carrier overseas hotline with passport details — reviews say this is **top priority**.
</FaqItem>

<FaqItem question="Insurance without a police report?">
  **Unlikely.** Theft/loss claims usually need **police case number and original report**, plus IMEI and receipt.
</FaqItem>

<FaqItem question="Can I recover a lost phone in Taichung?">
  Recoveries reported via **MRT lost & found (02-2720-8889), Uber, hotel**, taxi, hotel. **Find My** + police cooperation cases exist — block SIM first.
</FaqItem>

<FaqItem question="Police report on weekends?">
  **district police (110)** 24/7 — reports possible on weekends.
</FaqItem>

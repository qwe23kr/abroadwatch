## FAQ

<FaqItem question="Can I fly home the same day I lose my passport in Da Nang?">
  **Almost never.** Emergency passports take **2–3 business days**; **Embassy of Korea in Vietnam (Hanoi)** intake is **weekdays ~09:00–16:00 (varies by mission)** only. **Reschedule your flight +1–2 days** first.
</FaqItem>

<FaqItem question="How long does an emergency passport take?">
  Usually **2–3 business days** after intake. Always call **+84-24-3831-5111** to confirm current processing time.
</FaqItem>

<FaqItem question="What if I don't have a passport photo?">
  Photo shops near embassy — confirm 4×6 cm and background color
</FaqItem>

<FaqItem question="Can I get help on weekends?">
  **police station (113)**: 24/7 in major cities (varies) for reports. **Embassy of Korea in Vietnam (Hanoi)** passport intake: **weekdays ~09:00–16:00 (varies by mission)** — closed weekends/holidays. Weekend loss: police report → reschedule flight → **call mission next business day**.
</FaqItem>

## FAQ

<FaqItem question="Most common scam in Ho Chi Minh City?">
  Fake taxi / Grab — Airport/station **rigged meters, long routes**. Fake Grab badges. Book only in official Grab app.
</FaqItem>

<FaqItem question="Pay more on the spot to resolve?">
  **Do not pay more.** Reviews: move to safety, save evidence, then **card issuer and police**.
</FaqItem>

<FaqItem question="Common scams in Ho Chi Minh City?">
  Bar touts, rigged taxis, exchange/ATM traps, tour upsells, fake police — see scam types in this guide.
</FaqItem>

<FaqItem question="Can I get money back?">
  **Card disputes and insurance** sometimes recover funds. Cash is harder but **reports and receipts** help.
</FaqItem>

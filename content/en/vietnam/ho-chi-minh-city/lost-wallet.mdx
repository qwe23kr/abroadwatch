## FAQ

<FaqItem question="Is blocking cards enough?">
  **Block cards + police loss report** is standard. Reviews say **original report** needed for insurance and ATM disputes.
</FaqItem>

<FaqItem question="Cash only — still need police?">

</FaqItem>

<FaqItem question="Lost & found in Ho Chi Minh City?">
  Check **Grab lost & found, hotel, cafe, transport desk** — wallet returns in 1–3 days reported. Block cards first.

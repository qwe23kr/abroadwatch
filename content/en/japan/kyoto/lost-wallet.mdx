## FAQ

<FaqItem question="Is blocking cards enough?">
  **Block cards + police loss report** is standard. Reviews say **original report** needed for insurance and ATM disputes.
</FaqItem>

<FaqItem question="Cash only — still need police?">
  If cash loss is **covered by insurance**, you need a **police certificate**. Report anyway to prevent further loss.
</FaqItem>

<FaqItem question="Lost & found in Kyoto?">
  Check **JR lost & found (050-2016-1603), hotel, taxi** — wallet returns in 1–3 days reported. Block cards first.
</FaqItem>

## FAQ

<FaqItem question="Can I fly home the same day I lose my passport in Tokyo?">
  **Almost never.** Emergency passports usually take **1–2 business days**, and consulate intake is **weekdays 09:00–16:00** only. Same-day return is extremely rare — **reschedule your flight +1–2 days** first.
</FaqItem>

<FaqItem question="How long does an emergency passport take?">
  **1–2 business days** after intake is typical. Common pattern: 30 min at koban → 1–2 hr embassy wait → **pick up next morning**. Same-day issue happens but always call **+81-3-3455-2601** to confirm.
</FaqItem>

<FaqItem question="What if I don't have a passport photo?">
  Many travelers shoot **same day** at **passport photo shops** near the embassy (Azabu-Juban, Shinjuku). Konbini/kiosk prints often **fail spec** — confirm **passport size (3.5×4.5 cm, within 6 months)**.
</FaqItem>

<FaqItem question="Can I get help on weekends?">
  **Police koban**: 24/7 loss reports. **Embassy passport intake**: **weekdays 09:00–16:00** only — **closed Sat/Sun and Japanese holidays**. Weekend loss: koban report → reschedule flight → **call embassy next business day**.
</FaqItem>

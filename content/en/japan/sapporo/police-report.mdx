## FAQ

<FaqItem question="Language barrier?">
  **Translation app**, hotel staff. Say **for travel insurance**.
</FaqItem>

<FaqItem question="Which police station?">
  **Jurisdiction of the incident location** — not necessarily near your hotel.
</FaqItem>

<FaqItem question="Same-day certificate?">
  In-person filing often gets **same-day** case number and stamp. Online-only often **not accepted** for insurance (e.g. Taiwan).
</FaqItem>

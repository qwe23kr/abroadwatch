## FAQ

<FaqItem question="Can I fly home the same day I lose my passport in Bangkok?">
  **Almost never.** Emergency passports take **2–3 business days**; **Embassy of Korea in Thailand** intake is **weekday intake ~09:00–12:00 (varies by mission)** only. **Reschedule your flight +1–2 days** first.
</FaqItem>

<FaqItem question="How long does an emergency passport take?">
  Usually **2–3 business days** after intake. Always call **+66-2-247-7537** to confirm current processing time.
</FaqItem>

<FaqItem question="What if I don't have a passport photo?">
  Passport photo shops near embassy — confirm size and background with mission
</FaqItem>

<FaqItem question="Can I get help on weekends?">
  **Tourist Police or local station**: Tourist Police 1155 24/7 for reports. **Embassy of Korea in Thailand** passport intake: **weekday intake ~09:00–12:00 (varies by mission)** — closed weekends/holidays. Weekend loss: police report → reschedule flight → **call mission next business day**.
</FaqItem>

## FAQ

<FaqItem question="Can I block the SIM without the phone?">
  **Yes.** Call carrier overseas hotline or Tourist Police 1155 with passport details — reviews say this is **top priority**.
</FaqItem>

<FaqItem question="Insurance without a police report?">
  **Unlikely.** Theft/loss claims usually need **police case number and original report**, plus IMEI and receipt.
</FaqItem>

<FaqItem question="Can I recover a lost phone in Pattaya?">
  Recoveries reported via **BTS/MRT lost & found, Grab, hotel front desk**, taxi, hotel. **Find My** + police cooperation cases exist — block SIM first.
</FaqItem>

<FaqItem question="Police report on weekends?">
  **Tourist Police or local station** Tourist Police 1155 24/7 — reports possible on weekends (Tourist Police 1155).
</FaqItem>
